<h3 aria-controls="ui-id-6" aria-expanded="false" aria-selected="false" class="js-views-accodion-group-header ui-accordion-header ui-corner-top ui-accordion-header-collapsed ui-corner-all ui-state-default ui-accordion-icons" id="ui-id-5" role="tab" tabindex="-1"><span class="ui-accordion-header-icon ui-icon ui-icon-plus"></span><a href="/node/297" hreflang="en">International Roaming</a></h3>
<div aria-hidden="true" aria-labelledby="ui-id-5" class="ui-accordion-content ui-corner-bottom ui-helper-reset ui-widget-content" id="ui-id-6" role="tabpanel" style="display: none;"><div class="more-info-views-views views-row">
<div class="views-field views-field-body"><span class="field-content"><p><strong>How do I enjoy free roaming services? </strong></p>
<p>Roaming service is now available in Malaysia and Indonesia as part of TPG free service trial. You will need to:</p>
<ol><li>Ensure your TPG SIM card is active for at least 24 hours while in Singapore before your trip to allow your SIM to be automatically updated with the latest parameters.<br/>
	 </li>
<li>Enable Data Roaming when you are in one of our free trial roaming destinations. <br/>
	 </li>
<li>Set the Network Selection in your mobile device to 'Automatic'. Alternatively, you may manually select YTL/ YES 4G in Malaysia or Smartfren in Indonesia. <br/>
	 </li>
<li>Ensure your mobile device is configured with <a href="https://www.tpgmobile.sg/community/topic/68-basic-mobile-device-settings-apn-settings/">TPG APN Settings</a>.</li>
</ol><p><br/>
The list of devices supported on YTL / YES 4G network can be found <a href="https://www.yes.my/supported-devices">here</a>. For Smartfren, the list can be found <a href="https://www.smartfren.com/id/daftar-hape-4g-yang-kompatibel-smartfren-4g/">here</a>.   <br/><br/><br/><strong>What is included in the roaming plan? </strong></p>
<p>You can enjoy free unlimited data roaming in Malaysia and Indonesia at 1mbps. If you are using a <a href="https://www.tpgmobile.sg/community/topic/74-tpg-volte-supported-devices/">TPG VoLTE supported mobile device</a>, you will be able to make and receive calls to Singapore mobile and fixed lines from your existing service trial plan and all incoming SMS will be free. </p>
<p><br/><strong>Are outgoing calls to Malaysian/Indonesian mobile and fixed lines supported? </strong><br/><br/>
Roaming calls to Malaysia and Indonesia mobile and fixed lines will be available in the near future.<br/><br/><br/><strong>What are the charges if I have exceeded my plan limits? </strong></p>
<p>If you have exceeded the usage, the usage type will be barred for the remainder of the monthly billing period except for Data. </p>
<p style="margin:0cm 0cm 0.0001pt"><br/><strong>Will you be extending unlimited data roaming to other countries?</strong></p>
<p>Yes! We will be launching more exciting roaming products. Do keep a lookout on this page for the latest update. </p>

<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>This Service Trial is initially available to pre-registered users with Singapore NRIC and Permanent Pass holders 18 years old and above. This trial offer may be withdrawn at any time subject to market conditions.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Minimum Term</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>TPG Mobile Services are supplied on a rolling month to month basis. Customers are permitted to terminate the acquisition of the Service at any time, but you will forfeit any payments already made. TPG encourages the active use of our Mobile SIM cards. If TPG notices that there is no data usage for a continuous period of 30 days, TPG reserves the right to terminate the contract and recover your allotted number.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Excluded Usage</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>You can only use the service for usage types that are included as part of the plan. Any other usage types such as Calls &amp; SMS to International Numbers and MMS are not supported. If you exceed your plan’s inclusion for a specific usage type, that usage type will be barred for the remainder of the monthly billing period except for data. TPG will send you notification via SMS when you have exceeded your usage limit.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Mandatory Goods</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>It is not a requirement of TPG Mobile Services that customers acquire handsets or other equipment from TPG. However, you must have a compatible mobile handset to be able to use this service.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Fair use Policy</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>All TPG mobile plans are for personal use only and may not be used for commercial purposes or in a manner that may adversely affect the mobile network. In particular the services may not be used for wholesaling, reselling, SIM boxing or any other commercial purposes. If TPG reasonably believes that you are using the service for commercial purposes in breach of this clause, or in a manner that may adversely affect the mobile network, TPG may immediately suspend the mobile service and may provide 24 hours’ notice of termination of the mobile service. When your daily data usage exceeds 2GB, your speeds will be capped at 1Mbps for the remainder of the day. This plan is optimized for Smartphones and Tablets. Use in other devices is not supported and may see lower network speeds.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Coverage</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>
<p><span><span><span><span><span><span>Customers can only use the service when they use a 4G-ready (LTE 2300MHz/900MHz) handset or device in coverage areas. When customers are not within a TPG coverage area or their handset or device is not 4G-ready (LTE 2300MHz/900MHz), the TPG mobile service cannot be used. When inside the coverage areas, actual speeds vary due to factors such as location, distance from the base station, local terrain, user numbers, hardware &amp; software configuration and download source/upload destination. Currently, TPG does not have any coverage in tunnels.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p> </p>
<p><span><span><span><strong><span><span><span>Voice and Public Emergency Calling:</span></span></span></strong></span></span></span></p>

If you do not use a compatible VoLTE device, voice calls including calls to emergency services will not work.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p></span></div><span class="views-field views-field-field-sub-views"><span class="field-content"></span></span>

<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>Yes, you can authorise a proxy to pick up your SIM card on your behalf. Please complete and sign this <a href="/sites/tpgmobile/files/media/2019-01/authorisation_letter_v2.pdf"><font color="blue">authorisation form</font></a>. Your proxy must bring this form together with their original NRIC and your original NRIC when picking up the SIM card.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>4. <p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>Your TPG SIM card will be activated automatically when you pick it up from one of our pick up locations.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>5. <ul><li style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 0.0001pt 36pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>The recurring charges for TPG Mobile service are payable monthly in advance. Payment options are GIRO (DBS &amp; OCBC banks only), Visa or Mastercard.</span></span></span></span></span></span></li>
<li style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt 36pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>We will email you an invoice before we charge you for your monthly recurring charges</span></span></span></span></span></span></li>
</ul><p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>6. Can I use the service for anything else other than what’s included in the plan?</span></span></span></b></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>You can only use the service for usage types that are included as part of the plan. Any other usage types such as Calls &amp; SMS to International Numbers and MMS are not supported. If you exceed your plan’s inclusion for a specific usage type, that usage type will be barred for the remainder of the monthly billing period.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>7. Is there any limitation pertaining to the Unlimited Data?</span></span></span></b></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>You can use the first 2GB per day (both downloads and uploads are counted) at our 4G speeds. After you’ve used 2GB in a day, your speeds will be capped at 1Mbps until 01:00 AM on the next day. While capped at 1Mbps, you’re free to use the service as much as you’d like.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>8. <p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>An auto-generated SMS notification will be sent to you upon 100% usage of your 2GB 4G full speed daily allowance. You can also track your TPG Mobile usage by logging into <a href="https://www.tpgmobile.sg/account" style="color:#0563c1; text-decoration:underline">My Account</a>. </span></span></span></span></span></span><b style="font-family: Calibri, sans-serif; font-size: 11pt;"> </b></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>9. Can I use my TPG mobile overseas? (International Roaming)</span></span></span></b></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>Roaming service is now available in Malaysia and Indonesia as part of TPG free service trial. More information on how to enjoy free roaming service can be found <a href="hhttps://www.tpgmobile.sg/community/topic/100-international-roaming/">here</a>. </span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"> </p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><b><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>10. I lost my phone and/or my SIM card </span></span></span></b></span></span></span></p>
<p style="margin-bottom:.0001pt; margin:0cm 0cm 8pt"><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'>If you lose your phone or your SIM card, please let us know as soon as possible via email at support@tpgmobile.sg. We'll confirm your replacement SIM Card order, then you can pick up your replacement SIM card from any of the available TPG Sim Card pick-up locations.</span></span></span></span></span></span><span style="font-size:11pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span style="font-size:13.0pt"><span style="line-height:107%"><span style='font-family:"Arial",sans-serif'> A $10 SIM replacement fee will be charged.</span></span></span></span></span></span></p>
