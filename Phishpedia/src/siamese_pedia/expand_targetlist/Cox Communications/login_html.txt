id="ra-sa-container"><h2>Solution</h2><div class="wide-rounded"> <div class="content"> <p>Use the following table to resolve common login errors with the Cox website.<br/>  </p> <table border="0" class="data-table data-table-simple" style="text-align: center;"> <thead> <tr> <th>Cause of the Problem</th> <th>Solution</th> </tr> </thead> <tbody> <tr> <td> <p><strong>User ID</strong> and <strong>Password</strong> haven't been created yet.</p> </td> <td> <p>Follow the steps in <a href="/residential/support/coxcom-account-registration-and-preferences.html">Cox.com Account Registration and Preferences</a> to set up your User ID and Password.</p> </td> </tr> <tr> <td> <p><strong>Password</strong> was entered with incorrect capitalization.</p> </td> <td> <p>The password is case sensitive. Meaning "WizardOfOz#9" is different from "WIZARDOFOZ#9" or "wizardofoz#9."</p> <ol> <li>Make sure the <strong>CapsLock</strong> and <strong>NumLock</strong> keys on the computer keyboard are unlocked.</li> <li>Re-enter your password.</li> </ol> </td> </tr> <tr> <td> <p><strong>Password</strong> was forgotten.</p> </td> <td> <ol> <li>Click the <a href="https://idm.east.cox.net/selfservice2/resetpassword.action?execution=e1s1" target="_blank">Forgot Password?</a><strong> </strong>link in the Sign In box on www.cox.com.</li> <li>Enter your <strong>User ID</strong>.</li> <li>Follow the instructions to reset your password. For security, you will enter your <strong>Secret Answer</strong> to the <strong>Secret Question</strong>.</li> <li>Return to the Sign In box and log in with the new password.</li> </ol> </td> </tr> <tr> <td> <p><strong>Password</strong> lock out</p> </td> <td> <p>Several login attempts in a row is perceived by the system as a security threat, such as a possible break in. The system "locks out" additional login attempts. This lock out is intended to block unauthorized access to your account. If your login fails after several attempts, click the<strong> </strong><a href="https://idm.east.cox.net/selfservice2/resetpassword.action?finalview=" target="_blank">Forgot Password?</a> link to reset your password.</p> <p class="note-info">Cox cannot undo or remove a "lock out" that is in process.</p> </td> </tr> <tr> <td> <p><strong>User ID</strong> was forgotten.</p> </td> <td> <ol> <li>Click the <a href="https://idm.east.cox.net/selfservice2/lookupuserid.action?execution=e2s1" target="_blank">Forgot User ID</a> link in the <strong>Sign In</strong> box.</li> <li> <p>For security you will provide your Cox 16-digit <strong>account number</strong>, <strong>PIN</strong>, or the last four digits of your social security number and the <strong>Secret Answer</strong> to your <strong>Secret Question</strong>.<br/> </p> <p><strong>Result</strong>: The User ID of the account holder displays.<br/> </p> <p class="note-info">The account holder can look up the user ID of an authorized user by clicking My Connection on the homepage, My Account, then Manage Users.</p> </li> </ol> </td> </tr> <tr> <td> <p><strong>Secret Answer </strong>was forgotten.</p> </td> <td> <ol> <li>If the Secret Answer has been forgotten, contact <a href="https://www.cox.com/residential/contactus.html" target="_blank">Cox Customer Support</a> to either reset the Password or look up the User ID so you can log in and update the Secret Answer.</li> <li>Return to the Cox homepage and <strong>Sign In</strong> with your User ID and Password.</li> <li>Click <strong>My Account</strong>, then <strong>Edit Secret Question</strong>. Select a new Secret Question and enter a new Secret Answer.</li> </ol> </td> </tr> </tbody> </table> </div> </div></div> </div> </div> </div> </div> </div> <div class="reference parbase section"><div
