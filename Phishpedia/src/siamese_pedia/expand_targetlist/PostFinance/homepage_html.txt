<small class="rich_list_item--pretitle">13. September 2019</small>
<h3 class="rich_list_item--title">Bernadette Koch appointed as a new Member of the Board of Directors of PostFinance</h3>
<div class="rich_list_item--content">The extraordinary PostFinance Ltd General Meeting has elected Bernadette Koch, who was nominated by the Swiss Post Ltd Board of Directors, as a new member of the PostFinance Ltd Board of Directors. She succeeds Marco Durrer, who has stood down from the Board of Directors. In Bernadette Koch, PostFinance is gaining someone with in-depth knowledge of the Swiss banking landscape and who has a broad, cross-sector network at her disposal. </div>
</a>
</li>
<li>
<a class="rich_list_item rich_list_item-newslist--item" href="/en/about-us/media/newsroom/press-releases/interim-results-for-2019-swiss-post-needs-new-sources-of-income.html">
<small class="rich_list_item--pretitle">29. August 2019</small>
<h3 class="rich_list_item--title">Interim results for 2019: Swiss Post needs new sources of income</h3>
<div class="rich_list_item--content">Lower interest income from PostFinance and continually falling letter volumes had an impact on Swiss Post figures in the first half of 2019. With a profit of 193 million francs, the Group’s earnings were 25 million francs lower year-on-year. The operating profit (EBIT) was also lower. Swiss Post Head of Finance, Alex Glanzmann, sees a need for action. </div>
</a>
